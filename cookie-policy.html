Cookie Policy

How ExpatLawyerSpain uses cookies and analytics technologies.

Last updated: 20 May 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how ExpatLawyerSpain uses cookies and similar technologies on expatlawyerspain.com.

ExpatLawyerSpain is a directory and enquiry-routing website for English-speaking people looking for lawyers in Spain. We use limited cookies and analytics tools to keep the website working, understand how visitors use it, and improve the service.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They can help a website work properly, remember basic preferences, measure traffic, or understand how visitors move through a site.

Some cookies are necessary for a website to function. Others, such as analytics cookies, are used to understand visitor behaviour and improve the website.

2. Types of cookies we may use

TypePurposeExamples
Strictly necessary cookiesUsed for security, routing, page delivery and basic site functionality.Cloudflare security or performance technologies.
Analytics cookiesUsed to understand how people find and use the website, which pages are visited, and where improvements are needed.Google Analytics and Cloudflare Web Analytics.
Preference cookiesUsed only if we add features that remember visitor preferences.Language or display preferences, if added later.

3. Analytics tools

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and user behaviour, such as pages visited, approximate location, device type and referral source. Google may process this information on servers outside Spain or the European Economic Area.

Google Analytics helps us understand which guides are useful, how people find ExpatLawyerSpain, and where the website needs improvement.

Cloudflare Web Analytics and security

We use Cloudflare services to help deliver, secure and measure the website. Cloudflare may process technical information such as IP address, browser type, page request, device information and traffic patterns.

Important: This page improves transparency, but it is not a full cookie consent system by itself. If we add or expand non-essential analytics or marketing cookies, we may need a consent banner or consent-management tool.

4. Third-party cookies

Some cookies or similar technologies may be set by third-party providers whose tools are used on the website. These providers have their own privacy and cookie policies.

  • Google Analytics: Google Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare: Cloudflare Privacy Policy

5. How to manage cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies, delete existing cookies, or receive a warning before cookies are stored.

Blocking some cookies may affect how websites work. Strictly necessary cookies may still be required for basic security and page delivery.

6. Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy if we add new tools, change how analytics works, or make changes to the website. The latest version will always be published on this page.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy, contact us at hello@expatlawyerspain.com.
