How We Verify Firms

Last updated: July 2026

Every firm on ExpatLawyerSpain is checked by a person before it appears in the directory. This page sets out what that check covers, what the labels mean, and what gets a firm removed.

What we check before a firm is listed

  • It's a real, practising law firm. We confirm the firm exists, is active, and does the work it claims — checked against the firm's own published services, not a form it filled in.
  • English-language service is genuine. The firm serves clients in English — a real English-speaking team, not a machine-translated website. This is the point of the directory, so it's the first thing we check.
  • Specialties and coverage match reality. The legal areas and cities shown on a firm's card reflect what the firm actually publishes and practises. We don't list a firm under a specialty because it would be convenient for our pages.
  • Ratings are shown as published. Where a card shows a Google rating, it's the firm's public Google rating and review count at the time we last checked. We never edit, weight or invent ratings, and firms cannot pay to change them.

What the labels mean

Verified means the checks above have been done. Featured currently marks firms that have engaged directly with ExpatLawyerSpain. No firm can pay to rank above another in search results — ordering reflects relevance to your filters, nothing else.

Firms that don't respond get removed

A listing is only useful if the firm answers. When we route an enquiry, the firm is expected to respond within 2–5 working days; if it doesn't, we route the enquiry to the next best-fit firm. A firm that repeatedly ignores client enquiries is removed from the directory — being listed is conditional on treating enquiries seriously.

What we are not

ExpatLawyerSpain is not a law firm and doesn't provide legal advice. We're a directory and enquiry-routing service: free for users, neutral between firms, with no commissions that affect which firm you see. The advice you get comes from the lawyer you choose — our job is making sure the firms you're choosing between are real, English-speaking and responsive.

Found something on a listing that's out of date? Tell us at hello@expatlawyerspain.com and we'll check it.
