Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

ExpatLawyerSpain.com ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Who we are

ExpatLawyerSpain.com is an online directory connecting English-speaking expats in Spain with local lawyers. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice. For questions about this policy, contact us at hello@expatlawyerspain.com.

2. What data we collect

When you submit an enquiry through our contact form, we collect:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (optional)
  • Your legal need and location
  • Any additional information you provide in your message

We do not collect any payment information. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.

3. How we use your data

We use the information you submit solely to:

  • Connect you with a suitable English-speaking lawyer in Spain
  • Forward your enquiry to one or more relevant law firms listed in our directory
  • Respond to any follow-up questions you have about our service

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling or any purpose other than connecting you with legal help.

4. Who we share your data with

When you submit an enquiry, your details are shared with the relevant law firm(s) listed on our directory so they can follow up with you directly. By submitting an enquiry, you consent to this sharing.

We also use the following third-party services to operate this website:

  • Formspree - processes and delivers form submissions. Data is temporarily stored on their servers (US-based, Privacy Shield compliant). See Formspree Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare Web Analytics - privacy-friendly website analytics. No cookies used, no personal data collected, no cross-site tracking. We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand aggregate website usage, including page views, traffic sources, countries, devices and browser information. We do not send names, contact details, phone numbers, message contents or other personal enquiry information to Cloudflare Web Analytics. See Cloudflare Web Analytics.
  • Google Fonts - used to load website fonts. May log your IP address. See Google Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

5. Legal basis for processing

We process your data on the basis of your consent, given when you submit an enquiry through our contact form. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us at hello@expatlawyerspain.com.

6. How long we keep your data

We retain enquiry data for a maximum of 12 months, after which it is deleted. You may request earlier deletion at any time.

7. Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@expatlawyerspain.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Cloudflare Web Analytics, our analytics provider, operates without cookies and is used only for aggregate website usage analytics. We do not use heatmaps, session recording, advertising cookies or cross-site tracking.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, contact us at hello@expatlawyerspain.com.
